## PHOTO RECAP BEST PRACTICES

### 📷 **PHOTO SELECTION TIPS**

1. **Variety is Key**
   - Mix wide shots and close-ups
   - Include people, details, and ambiance
   - Show different times of day
   - Capture various emotions

2. **Quality Over Quantity**
   - Choose only sharp, well-lit photos
   - Avoid duplicates of same moment
   - Select photos that tell a story
   - Ensure faces are clear and flattering

3. **Representation Matters**
   - Include different groups (family, friends, guests)
   - Show various ages in photos
   - Feature key moments from each part of event
   - Highlight different budget categories

4. **Balance Posed & Candid**
   - 40% posed/formal shots
   - 60% candid/natural moments
   - Mix of serious and fun photos
   - Include unexpected special moments

---

### 🎨 **DESIGN & EDITING TIPS**

1. **Consistent Editing Style**
   - Use same filter/preset across all photos
   - Maintain consistent brightness/contrast
   - Match color tones
   - Keep editing natural, not over-processed

2. **Text Overlay Best Practices**
   - Use readable fonts (size 24+ for headlines)
   - Ensure high contrast (dark text on light backgrounds)
   - Keep text concise and impactful
   - Place text in areas without important details

3. **Layout & Composition**
   - Leave white space—don't overcrowd
   - Use grids for organized look
   - Align elements properly
   - Create visual flow (top to bottom, left to right)

4. **Branding Elements**
   - Include event hashtag
   - Use consistent color scheme
   - Add date/location where relevant
   - Consider watermark for professional photos

---

### 📱 **PLATFORM-SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATIONS**

**Instagram Posts:**
- Square format (1080 x 1080px)
- Maximum 10 photos per carousel
- First photo should be most eye-catching
- Use all 30 hashtags in first comment

**Instagram Stories:**
- Vertical format (1080 x 1920px)
- 15-second video clips
- Use stickers (polls, questions, countdowns)
- Save highlights for permanent viewing

**Facebook Albums:**
- Landscape or square photos work best
- Can upload 50+ photos
- Tag people in photos (with permission)
- Create multiple albums by category

**WhatsApp Status:**
- Keep under 30 seconds for videos
- Use text sparingly (viewers on mobile)
- Post multiple times throughout day
- Respond to reactions and messages

---

### ⏰ **TIMING YOUR PHOTO RECAPS**

**Day After Event:**
- 3-5 "sneak peek" photos
- Quick thank you message
- Teaser for full album

**3-5 Days After:**
- Full photo album/carousel
- Detailed caption with gratitude
- Tag vendors and supporters

**One Week After:**
- Behind-the-scenes photos
- Budget transparency post with visuals
- Vendor appreciation posts

**One Month After:**
- Professional photos released
- Video highlights (if available)
- Final thank you post
- Memory reflection post

---

### 💬 **CAPTION WRITING TIPS**

1. **Start with Emotion**
   - "We're still on cloud nine!"
   - "Our hearts are SO full!"
   - "Reliving this perfect day!"

2. **Express Gratitude**
   - Specific thank yous
   - Mention number of supporters
   - Acknowledge specific contributions

3. **Tell the Story**
   - Brief narrative of the day
   - Special moments highlighted
   - Unexpected surprises

4. **Include Details**
   - Date and location
   - Number of guests
   - Budget information (if doing transparency)
   - Vendor credits

5. **End with Call-to-Action**
   - "Swipe to see more!"
   - "Tag yourself if you're in these!"
   - "Drop a ❤️ if you were there!"
   - "Comment your favorite moment!"
