## TIPS FOR MILESTONE ANNOUNCEMENTS

### 🎯 **FREQUENCY & TIMING**

**How Often to Announce:**
- Don't overdo it—celebrate significant milestones only
- Space announcements at least 5-7 days apart
- Save minor updates for social media stories

**Best Times to Post:**
- Weekday mornings (8-10 AM) for email
- Weekday evenings (7-9 PM) for social media  
- Weekends for major milestone celebrations

---

### ✍️ **CONTENT BEST PRACTICES**

**Always Include:**
✅ Specific numbers (raised, supporters, percentage)
✅ What the milestone means
✅ Thank you to supporters
✅ What's next / call to action
✅ Event reminder (date, location)

**Avoid:**
❌ Too much detail—keep it concise
❌ Complaining about how much is left to raise
❌ Pressuring people who haven't pledged
❌ Comparing pledge amounts

---

### 📸 **VISUAL ELEMENTS**

**Great Visual Ideas:**
- Progress bars showing percentage
- Thermometer graphics  
- Number highlights (50%, 100 supporters, etc.)
- Behind-the-scenes photos
- Vendor previews
- Countdown graphics

**Design Tips:**
- Use your event colors consistently
- Include your event hashtag
- Make numbers BIG and easy to read
- Use emojis for visual interest  
- Keep text minimal on graphics

---

### 💬 **TONE & VOICE**

**Effective Tone:**
- Excited and grateful
- Authentic and genuine
- Inclusive ("WE did this together!")
- Forward-looking ("What's next")

**Avoid:**
- Entitled or demanding
- Overly formal/stuffy
- Desperate or begging
- Negative or complaining
