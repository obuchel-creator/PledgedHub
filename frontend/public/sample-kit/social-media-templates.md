## TIPS FOR SOCIAL MEDIA SUCCESS:

### Posting Frequency:
- **Initial Campaign:** Daily posts for first week
- **Mid-Campaign:** 3-4 posts per week
- **Final Week:** Daily countdown posts
- **Event Day:** Multiple stories/posts throughout the day

### Best Times to Post:
- **Facebook:** 1-4 PM weekdays
- **Instagram:** 11 AM - 2 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Twitter:** 12-3 PM, 5-6 PM
- **WhatsApp Status:** Early morning (7-9 AM) or evening (7-10 PM)

### Engagement Boosters:
1. **Use Emojis** - Makes posts more eye-catching
2. **Ask Questions** - Encourages comments
3. **Tag Supporters** - When appropriate and with permission
4. **Use Local Hashtags** - Reach your community
5. **Respond Quickly** - Reply to all comments and messages
6. **Cross-Post** - Share across all platforms for maximum reach
7. **Stories > Feed** - Stories get more casual engagement

### Content Mix (80/20 Rule):
- **80%** Value/Updates/Thank Yous/Behind-the-Scenes
- **20%** Direct asks for pledges

### Visual Guidelines:
- **Use Consistent Colors** - Match your event theme
- **High-Quality Photos** - Clear, well-lit images only
- **Brand Your Graphics** - Add event name/hashtag to images
- **Keep Text Readable** - Large fonts, high contrast
- **Include Faces** - Posts with people get more engagement
