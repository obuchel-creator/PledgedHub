### Step 1: Database (1 min)
```powershell
node backend\scripts\migration-payout-system.js
```

### Step 2: Configuration (1 min)
```
Update backend\.env:
AIRTEL_MERCHANT_ID=your_actual_id
PLATFORM_COMMISSION_PERCENT=10
```

### Step 3: Server (1 min)
```powershell
npm run dev
```

Cron job starts automatically

## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS

### Why This Design
1. **Transparent fees** - Creators know their take-home
2. **Bank optimization** - Lowest fees automatically used
3. **Automation** - No manual monthly processing
4. **Compliance** - Full audit trail for URA
5. **Scale** - Handles 1,000+ creators
6. **Security** - Role-based access control
7. **Flexibility** - Configurable commission rates

### Why Monthly Automation
- Gives 30 days to collect pledges
- Runs on 1st of month (safe day)
- Admin has 5 days to review & transfer
- Prevents rushed payments
- Time for weekend bank processing

### Why 10% Commission
- Covers platform costs
- Supports 24/7 operations
- Funds development
- Competitive vs industry (15-20%)
- Can be adjusted per tier
