## TIPS FOR WRITING THANK YOU LETTERS

### ✍️ **PERSONALIZATION IS KEY**

1. **Use Their Name** - Not just in greeting, but throughout the letter
2. **Reference Your Relationship** - Mention shared history, inside jokes, specific memories
3. **Be Specific About Their Contribution** - State the exact amount and what it's funding
4. **Acknowledge Their Unique Role** - Early supporter? Large donor? Friend who spread the word?
5. **Match Their Communication Style** - Formal for professional, casual for friends

---

### 📝 **WRITING BEST PRACTICES**

1. **Send Promptly**
   - Initial pledge thank you: Within 24 hours
   - Collection thank you: Within 48 hours of receiving payment
   - Post-event thank you: Within one week after event

2. **Be Genuine**
   - Write from the heart
   - Avoid overly formal language (unless appropriate)
   - Express real emotions
   - Don't be afraid to show vulnerability

3. **Keep It Concise**
   - Aim for 200-400 words for most thank you letters
   - Make every word count
   - Get to the point quickly
   - End strong

4. **Include Practical Information**
   - Always include pledge details
   - Provide collection information
   - Include event details
   - Offer contact information

5. **Proofread Carefully**
   - Check spelling of names (crucial!)
   - Verify all amounts and dates
   - Read out loud to catch awkward phrasing
   - Have someone else review before sending

---

### 📧 **DELIVERY METHODS**

**Email** (Most Common)
- ✅ Fast and efficient
- ✅ Easy to include links and details
- ✅ Can track if opened
- ❌ Can feel impersonal

**Handwritten Note** (Most Personal)
- ✅ Shows extra effort and care
- ✅ Feels more personal and special
- ✅ Can be kept as keepsake
- ❌ Time-consuming for many supporters
- **Best for:** Major donors, close family, VIP supporters

**WhatsApp/Text** (Quick & Personal)
- ✅ Immediate and conversational
- ✅ Great for close friends/family
- ✅ Allows for back-and-forth dialogue
- ❌ Less formal, not suitable for all
- **Best for:** Close friends, younger supporters, quick acknowledgments

**Phone Call** (Ultimate Personal Touch)
- ✅ Most personal option
- ✅ Allows real conversation
- ✅ Can express emotion with voice
- ❌ Time-intensive
- **Best for:** Major donors, very close family, elderly supporters who appreciate calls
