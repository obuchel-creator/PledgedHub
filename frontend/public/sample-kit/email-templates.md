## Tips for Using These Templates:

1. **Personalize Each Email** - Use the supporter's name and reference their specific pledge amount
2. **Send at the Right Time** - Space out updates (weekly or bi-weekly) to avoid overwhelming supporters
3. **Include Visuals** - Add photos, progress bars, or graphics to make emails more engaging
4. **Mobile-Optimize** - Keep emails concise and easy to read on phones
5. **Include Clear CTAs** - Make it easy for supporters to take action (pay pledge, RSVP, share updates)
6. **Proofread Carefully** - Double-check all amounts, dates, and names before sending
7. **Test Send First** - Send a test email to yourself to check formatting
8. **Track Engagement** - Note which emails get the best responses to improve future communications
