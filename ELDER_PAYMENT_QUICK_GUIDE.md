# Elder-Friendly Payment Guide

**Simple Ways to Pay Your Pledge**

---

## 🏠 Choose Your Preferred Method

### 📱 Method 1: Phone Dial Code (EASIEST!)

#### For MTN Users (077, 078 numbers)

```
1. Dial *165# on your phone
2. Press 4 for "My Wallet"
3. Press 3 for "My Approvals"
4. You will see payment request
5. Press OK and enter your PIN
✅ DONE!
```

#### For Airtel Users (070, 075 numbers)

```
1. Dial *185# on your phone
2. Press 1 for "Airtel Money"
3. Press 2 for "Make Payment"
4. Enter merchant code: 12345
5. Press OK and enter your PIN
✅ DONE!
```

---

### 🌐 Method 3: Website (WITH PICTURES)

#### Step 1: Open Website
- Go to: www.pledgehub.ug/pay
- Click the BIG GREEN button

#### Step 2: Enter Phone Number
- Type your phone number
- Example: 0772345678
- Click NEXT (big button)

#### Step 3: Enter Pledge Number
- Type your pledge number
- Example: 123
- Click NEXT

#### Step 4: Confirm Amount
- Check the amount is correct
- Click PAY NOW (big green button)

#### Step 5: Approve on Phone
- Dial *165# (MTN) or *185# (Airtel)
- Follow the steps shown on screen
- Enter your PIN
- ✅ DONE!

## 🆘 Need Help?

### Common Questions

**Q: What if I dial the wrong code?**
A: No problem! Just dial the correct code again. Your money is safe.

**Q: How do I know my payment was received?**
A: You will get a text message saying "Payment received! Thank you."

**Q: What if my phone is off?**
A: Turn on your phone and follow the steps. The payment request will still be there.

**Q: I forgot my PIN**
A: Call your mobile money provider:
   - MTN: 100 (toll-free)
   - Airtel: 175 (toll-free)

**Q: Can someone help me?**
A: YES! Call 0800-753343 anytime during working hours.

## 🔒 Safety Tips

### Your Money is Safe Because:
- ✅ You must enter YOUR PIN (only you know it)
- ✅ You approve on YOUR phone
- ✅ You get SMS confirmation
- ✅ PledgeHub never asks for your PIN
- ✅ All payments are secure

### Never:
- ❌ Share your PIN with anyone
- ❌ Approve payments you don't recognize
- ❌ Give phone to strangers
- ❌ Click suspicious links in SMS

### Always:
- ✅ Check amount before approving
- ✅ Keep SMS receipts
- ✅ Call helpline if confused: 0800-753343
- ✅ Ask questions - no question is silly!
